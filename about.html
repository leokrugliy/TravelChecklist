About This Site

Traveling is exciting — but packing? Not so much.
We’ve all had that moment when we arrive at the airport, train station, or campsite and realize we forgot something important. That’s exactly why this little site exists.
It’s a simple tool to help you create a packing checklist in seconds — no logins, no annoying popups, no distractions. Just pick your trip type (like flying, hiking, road trip, or traveling with kids), and we’ll give you a quick, smart list of things you probably don’t want to forget.
The idea came from personal experience: I love traveling, but I hate making packing lists every time from scratch. So I made this tiny helper — for myself at first, and now it’s here for you too.
This site will always be free and lightweight. If it helped you — feel free to share it with a fellow traveler.
Safe travels! 🌍✈️
